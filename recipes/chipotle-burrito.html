Chipotle Burrito Bowl

Description

Mexican burrito bowl, with a nice spicy kick!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Chopped Onion
  • White Rice
  • Avacado
  • Chopped Jalapeno
  • Sour Cream
  • Chipotle seasoning

Directions

  1. Brown up ground beef in a pan and season with chipotle powder, adding chopped onion and jalapenos
  2. Boil water and cook up white rice
  3. Distribute beef mix evenly with rice
  4. Garnish with some sour cream and enjoy!
