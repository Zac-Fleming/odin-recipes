Protein Shake

Discription

Delicious shake with a great source of protein that can be enjoyed after a great workout. Protein powder and fruit can be switched out to your preference!

Ingredients

  • Greek yogurt (Flavor is up to you but I prefer Vanilla)
  • Blueberries
  • Banana
  • Milk
  • Protein Powder

Directions

  1. Add Banana and blue berries to blender
  2. Mix in one or two scoops of protein poweder
  3. add yogurt and milk
  4. Blend ingredients for 30-45 seconds
  5. Pour and enjoy !!
